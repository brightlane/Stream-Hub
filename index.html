How to embed Restream on your website

Why embedding matters

Embedding your live stream on your website keeps viewers on your domain, which improves SEO and engagement. Instead of sending traffic out to Twitch or YouTube, you can host the live video right on your site and keep it under your control.

Restream.io provides an embeddable player that you can paste into your HTML, WordPress, Wix, or Webflow page. When you go live, the player automatically updates so your embedded stream stays in sync with your public broadcast.

How to embed the player

Inside Restream.io, go to the “Stream” tab and click “Embed” to generate a code snippet. Copy that code and paste it into the HTML of your site where you want the live stream to appear. You can also adjust the width, height, and autoplay behavior to match your design.

For SEO, always add a descriptive heading above the embed (for example, “Watch our live gaming stream” or “Join our live church service”) so search engines understand what the embedded content represents.
